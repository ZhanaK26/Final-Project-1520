Here you will be able get a description of all the activites and events we have to offer. You can also choose your pick up times to retreive your gear and choose the time you would like particicipate in an event or activity.

This event will allow you and or your family to hike a two hour beginner trail.
Gear and supplies include: hiking backpack, weather-appropriate clothing (moisture-wicking and layers), hiking boots or shoes (choose day of pickup), variety of snacks (4 per hiker), water, map and compass, first-aid kit, and a knife or multi-tool.

This activity will allow you and or your family to fish in our huge lake with plenty of fish for everyone. Gear and supplies include: rod, reel,fishing line, tacle, tackle box, and hand tools.

This activity will allow you and or your family to go for an hour and thirty minute swim. Gear and supplies include: swim cap, wetsuit, goggles, cold water gear, flip flops, water shoes, and ear plugs.

This activity will allow your family to take up to two canoes out on the water at a time with an instructor. Gear and supplies include: canoe paddle (1 per paddler), extra paddle, 2 personal flotation devices (1 per paddler), seat, Knee pads, and yoke (with pads).

This is a family only activity for familys to bond while riding on our 3 three hour bike trail. Gear and supplies include: helmet, water bottle, tire levers, spare tube, pump, multi tool, seat bag,lights, lock, and two snacks.

This activity is fun for everyone. Everyone will lay out on their blankets and gaze at the stars and relax. Gear and supplies include: astronomy or star chart, flashlights,blankets (2 per family), binoculars, and an stronomer’s toolkit.
